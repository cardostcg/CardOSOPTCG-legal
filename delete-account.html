DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

Last updated June 17, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your CardOS OP TCG account and the personal data associated with it. CardOS OP TCG ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile app for One Piece Trading Card Game collectors that lets users track their card collection, graded slabs, wishlist, and portfolio value.

HOW TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from inside the app. No email request is required.

  1. Open the CardOS OP TCG app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account and the data listed below are permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

If you can no longer access the app, you may instead request deletion by emailing us at CardOSTCG@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. Please include the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

WHAT DATA IS DELETED

When you delete your account, we permanently remove the following:

  • Your authentication account and email address
  • Your card collection records
  • Your graded slab vault records
  • Your wishlist
  • Your portfolio value history
  • Uploaded slab photos and any other files stored under your account
  • Affiliate click records associated with your account

WHAT DATA IS KEPT

We do not retain personal data after account deletion. Once your account is deleted, the data listed above is removed from our systems.

Limited, non-identifying records may persist where required, including:

  • Anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer be linked to you
  • Records we are legally required to retain (for example, transaction or tax records related to a purchase), kept only for the period required by applicable law

Public reference data such as card catalog information is not personal data and is not affected by account deletion.

RETENTION PERIOD

Account and personal data deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation in the app. Where data must be retained for legal or regulatory reasons, it is kept only for the minimum period required by law and then deleted.

CONTACT

If you have questions about deleting your account or your data, contact us at CardOSTCG@gmail.com.
